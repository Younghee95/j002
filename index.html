About

Jo Younghee is a UX designer and founder of NINEFIVE, a digital design agency delivering end-to-end experience design across strategy, interface, and emerging technologies. With over two decades of hands-on industry experience, she has led complex, large-scale projects spanning technology, retail, and media sectors, collaborating with global organizations such as Samsung, LG, and Hyundai.

Her expertise lies in structuring user experiences at scale — translating abstract systems, data, and technologies into coherent, intuitive interfaces. She has built and directed multidisciplinary teams across UX, visual design, media, and XR, consistently delivering high-impact digital products and brand experiences.

Extending from this foundation, her recent work expands into XR UX as an advanced research domain. As a PhD researcher in Integrated Design at Yonsei University, she investigates how interaction design principles evolve within immersive environments — focusing on how scale influences perception and behavior in XR, and proposing adaptive interaction mechanisms that adjust sensitivity, distance, visual density, and information representation based on user context. She also teaches UX and interface design at Yonsei University, bridging industry practice with academic thinking.
